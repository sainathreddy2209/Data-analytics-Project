About the Project

üìä Project Overview

Strategic Product Placement Analysis is a data-driven project that explores how product positioning influences sales performance and consumer behavior. Using Tableau visualizations, the project uncovers patterns in placement strategies across retail stores, digital platforms, and media content to understand their impact on customer engagement and purchasing decisions.

‚ö† Problem Statement

Retail and media companies invest heavily in product placement strategies but lack clear insights into how these placements affect sales and consumer preferences. Although data on sales, placement locations, and customer demographics is available, it is rarely analyzed together visually, making it difficult to identify effective positioning strategies and optimize marketing efforts.

üí° Proposed Solution

This project provides an interactive Tableau-based analytics solution integrated into a web application. It visualizes relationships between product placement, sales trends, and consumer behavior, helping businesses identify high-impact placement strategies, understand customer preferences, and make data-driven decisions to maximize revenue and marketing effectiveness.
